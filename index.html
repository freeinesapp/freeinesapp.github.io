INES

INteractive model for Extrapolation of Survival and cost

INES for Windows

INES is a new open-access modeling tool powered by R, designed for healthcare professionals and investigators who are familiar with economic evaluation principles but not proficient in statistical packages like Excel or R. INES is specifically tailored for partitioned survival models (PSM), which are widely used in health technology assessment, particularly in late-stage and metastatic cancer.
